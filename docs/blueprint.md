# **App Name**: Buen Corte

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Display a curated selection of high-quality meat products, filterable by type and cut.
- Detailed Product View: Provide comprehensive product information including origin, preparation tips, and suggested wine pairings.
- Weight Selection: Allow users to specify the desired weight for each product with clear feedback of pricing, servings and final price.
- Shopping Cart: Enable users to add, view, and manage selected items in a cart before proceeding to checkout.
- AI Pairing Tool: A generative AI tool that suggests recipes and preparation tips based on selected cuts of meat. It can also advise on which products in the catalog would complement a chosen product

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep red (#8B0000) to evoke the rich color of high-quality meat, creating a sense of luxury and premium quality.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), offering a clean and appetizing backdrop that highlights the product imagery and reinforces the high-end feel.
- Accent color: Muted gold (#D4A27A), used sparingly for highlights, interactive elements, and key information to draw the eye without overwhelming the overall palette.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern serif font that will convey a sense of elegance
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif to provide clean readability for product details
- Use clear, outlined icons from Lucide to represent product features and actions (e.g., shopping cart, weight selection) with a modern, minimalist aesthetic.
- Employ a grid-based layout with generous spacing to showcase products, maintaining a clean and uncluttered aesthetic to guide users through the selection process.
